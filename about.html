About IIP Brothers

Some place between digital strategy, item outline and Light-foteed advancement

Every time my brother Igor and I are asked about how we came to develop “smart” electronics, we immediately answer that we’ve just made our old dream come true.

Here are five of our most successful projects:
- Portable charging devices of premium quality, 4SecondLife for iPhones.
- NFC P2P wristbands LIKERs, transferring data from one wristband to another.
- iPhone mood indicators iLighters.
- Wireless car parking sensors.
- Flagship Mojipic Wireless Emoji Display.

All our projects only became possible due to the fact that we love electronics and it just so happened that we decided to make our small contribution into this branch of the world industry. Our dream became a successful reality.

Our company’s history began in 2010 during one of our frequent trips to China. We were tourists at that time and the idea of IIP Brothers wasn’t born yet, but seeing the developing Chinese market with our own eyes, left us in awe.

Inspired by Shenzhen’s Silicon Valley in China we started thinking about inventing something of our own.

2013 became the beginning of our company. We registered our company IIP Brothers Limited in Hong Kong. And started to recruit our first team for the project 4SecondLife. We found talented programmers, engineers, designers and like-minded people.

Gradually our experience in software development and electronics grew and we switched to mass production. Inevitably, not all of our projects went smoothly, some of them failed or were simply not good enough, but we always managed to gain something useful from our failures.

Are we planning any new projects? Of course, we want to continue developing unique and innovative solutions in IoT area. The last 10 years are just the beginning.
